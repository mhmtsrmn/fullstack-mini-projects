Coffee

Overly consumed, underappreciated wonder of nature...

  • 850 AD - According to legend, the coffee plant was discovered in Ethiopia by a goat herder named Kaldi around, who observed increased physical activity in his goats after they consumed coffee beans.
  • 1500 - The coffee plant was first found in the mountains of Yemen. Then, it was exported to the rest of the world through the port of Mokha, Yemen.
  • 1600 - First cultivation in India (Chikmagalur)
  • 1616 - First cultivation in Europe
  • 1699 - First cultivation in Java
  • 1715-1730 - First cultivation in Caribbean (Cuba, Hispaniola, Jamaica, Puerto Rico)
  • 1720 - First cultivation in Dutch East Indies
  • 1730 - First cultivation in South America
  • 1865 - Roasted beans first sold on retail market (Pittsburgh)
  • 1950s - Important spray-drying techniques developed, which along with freeze drying are a method to create instant coffee
